## 3. Newly-introduced this session (small, contained)

These surfaced *because* of forward work and are the active debt to watch:

1. **‚öÝ Full `npm run sync` silently degrades articles (the real debt).**
   The current Notion Articles DB is a *subset* of the snapshot (323 vs 357 `en`),
   and some Notion article bodies are *shorter* than the committed sidecars. A full
   sync rewrites `articles.json` from Notion only ‚Äî it **dropped the 34 table-fixed
   articles** and **truncated 2 article bodies** (109‚Üí8, 127‚Üí17 blocks) this session.
   Both were caught and repaired (rows restored from git HEAD; sidecars reverted),
   and the trap is documented in CLAUDE.md. **Root cause: WP‚ÜîNotion divergence** ‚Äî
   the snapshot/sidecars hold richer WP-extract content than Notion now has. Until
   the Notion Articles DB is realigned, a full sync is *destructive to articles*.

2. **Event sidecar drift (minor).** 9 `event-en-*` block sidecars on disk but only
   6 events in `events.json` ‚Äî 3 orphans from events removed in Notion. Same
   snapshot-vs-Notion drift pattern; cosmetic (orphans aren't read) but untidy.

3. **Sustainability page is English-only (the gap you flagged).** The WP page is
   translated in 11 languages (~29‚Äì31K chars each), but only `en` was seeded to
   Notion; the other 13 locales fall back to English. The English-fallback was
   intended as graceful degradation but **masks** the missing translations. The
   translated content exists in WP and should be ported. *In progress.*

> Note: items 1‚Äì2 are **not** the block-duplication waste the prior audit named
> (that is fixed ‚Äî 0 dead dupes). They are a *different*, smaller divergence: the
> snapshot is now *ahead of* Notion, where before Notion writes were *ahead of*
> the snapshot. The fix direction is the same: make Notion the single source of
> truth by realigning the Articles DB to the WP-extract content.
